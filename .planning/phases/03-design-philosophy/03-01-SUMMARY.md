- Each principle includes: statement, rationale, DO examples, DON'T examples
- **"Warm" definition:** 3 dimensions (color, material, emotion)
- **"Simple" definition:** 3 boundaries (single-screen, multi-entry, accessible-but-clean)
- **Principle conflict resolution table** with 4 conflict pairs
- **Anti-pattern catalog:** 6 entries with severity, problem, example, and fix
